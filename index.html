About

Let me introduce myself.

      I am a front-end developer from Venezuela. I've always been a person who loves to learn. In 2020, I started self-learning the basics of front-end development in my free time while I was studying industrial engineering. Due to life circumstances, I had to drop out of university, but it was just an opportunity to continue the development path, something I enjoy doing.

      Thanks to the four years that I studied engineering, I have developed analysis skills suitable for a developer profile, results-oriented work, a well-built determination to achieve goals, and excellent communication skills.

      I like to challenge myself and push the boundaries of my abilities, which has helped me grow at the same time professionally and personally. I am an ambitious person who always seeks new challenges and tries not to get complacent with what I already know.

Profile

As a self-taught web developer with a background in industrial engineering, I bring a unique perspective to my work. My passion for technology and problem-solving led me to teach myself web development, where I quickly realized the potential of creating seamless user experiences through elegant design and intuitive functionality. I believe that my engineering background gives me an analytical approach to problem-solving, while my web development skills allow me to create engaging and user-friendly websites. I am always eager to learn new technologies and techniques to improve my skills and create innovative solutions for my clients.
